Sloe gin Negroni recipe

Whether you're after a gift or simply looking to treat yourself this year, make the most of a special bottle of sloe gin by knocking up this astringent and lip-smacking sloe gin Negroni

  Serves 4

  5 mins to prepare

  Strength: 6/10

Published by: AndrijaRakojevic

Rate this cocktail:

1 ratings


Ingredients

  • 100ml sloe gin
  • 100ml extra-dry vermouth
  • 100ml Campari
  • orange, thinly sliced
  • a few sprigs of thyme

Method

  1. Pour 100ml each sloe gin, extra-dry vermouth and Campari into a jug with plenty of ice cubes and a sprig of thyme; mix well until the jug feels cold.

  2. Strain into 4 tumblers filled with ice. Garnish with thin slices of blood orange and thyme sprigs.
